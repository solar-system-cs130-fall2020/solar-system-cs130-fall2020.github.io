About


This project is an interactive simulation that models planetary motion around a sun.

The simulation showcases the simple yet complex relationship heavenly bodies have with each other due to gravity. In addition, solar systems may be customized to the user's preference.


Features:

  • Fly around the solar system in a first-person view

  • Add and delete new planets to a solar system

  • Edit and customize a planet's attributes:

    • Terrain coloration

    • Terrain shape

    • Planet radius

    • Planet mass

    • Number of moons

  • Solar system overview viewing mode
